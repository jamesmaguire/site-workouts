* A1. Deadbug

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/Lbod9T8LvJc]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/Lbod9T8LvJc/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   Maintain natural curve in the spine
    -   Move slowly with core conrtansly enganged

* A2. Flat Lateral extension

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/dmF-RPmsN6g]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/dmF-RPmsN6g/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   This position is most easily obtained with a partner sitting on your
      feet. I have also done it hooking my lower foot under a 60 pound
      dumbbell on the bench.
    -   Maintain constant tension in the obliques as you lower yourself
      toward the floor. Bring yourself up to parallel or above parallel as
      you are able.

* A3. V-ups

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/EDm1xprt2sY]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/EDm1xprt2sY/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   Move under control with core engaged
    -   Aim hands to mid calf or above
    -   Move arms and legs simultaneously

   These workouts are to be preceded by the [[https://ultyresults.mykajabi.com/products/uap/categories/89306/posts/231481][Nonlinear Warmup]].

*  SAQ 1: Multi-directional

* A. Lateral Bound and Return

     {{{youtube(gxkZIPJYgro)}}}


     -   Like the Bound and Stick, but with an extra bound back to the
       starting point.
     -   Stick the landing as with the bound and stick

* B. Heiden Hops

     {{{youtube(u2e262ks7aY)}}}


* C. 5-10-5 lateral drill

     {{{youtube(lIq87ZhL8bE)}}}


     -   First 5 yards hips will be mostly perpendicular to line of movement.
       Use a big crossover push.
     -   Second 10 yards open the hips early coming in to the second turn
     -   Third five yards turn the hips and commit to accelerating though the
       cone.

*  SAQ 2: Linear and Disc

* A. Galloping

     -   Repeatedly leap off the same leg as high and as far as possible
     -   Use your arms for momentum

* B. Power Skips for Distance

     -   Aim for full hip extension and distance

* C. Forward Frog Jumps

     {{{youtube(nhDXP6h33qM)}}}


     -   the purpose of this drill is to get full extension of ankles, knees,
       and hips
     -   propel yourself explosively upward and a little bit forward

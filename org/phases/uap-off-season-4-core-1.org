* A1. Overhead Medicine Ball Carry
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/CCl-GOORm1Y]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/CCl-GOORm1Y/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - Bend side to side while walking and holding medicine ball overhead.
    - Bend to the side of the forward foot.
    - This can be done with a dumbbell if you have no medicine ball

* A2. One Arm Carry
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/h0QZyB5mvkc]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/h0QZyB5mvkc/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - Use a heavy dumbbell
    - Walk 20 yards and back

* A3. Prone Quadruped Bent Leg Lift
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/muJEa4AIEM0]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/muJEa4AIEM0/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - Keep back as stationary as possible while lifting the one leg up and back.
    - Heel should point to the sky
    - Squeeze your glute, do not arch your back
    - This exercise may not feel super hard. it does require focus to do it right. This exercise works at separating the hips and will prep you for things to come.

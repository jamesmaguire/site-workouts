     [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/p9QF2UiWjYE]]
     [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/p9QF2UiWjYE/hqdefault.jpg]]

     -   Keep arms straight

* A3. Barbell to body-weight Crunch

     [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/BxhNQDTxrgM]]
     [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/BxhNQDTxrgM/hqdefault.jpg]]

     -   Keep weight directly over head.
     -   Core should be engaged at all times (maybe just shy of fully sitting
       up).
     -   Feet are under a bench for stability but avoid using your legs/hip
       flexors.

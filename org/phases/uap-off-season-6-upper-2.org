*  A1. Paused High Incline Semi Sup DB Press

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/wzk2oykwTvA]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/wzk2oykwTvA/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   Pause at the bottom keeping tension in the chest, don't rest!
    -   Keep shoulders retracted

*  A2. 1 & 1/4 Inverse Row

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/ZiUmS7jbNkg]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/ZiUmS7jbNkg/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   Quarter rep at the top
    -   Get your chest to the bar for full shoulder retraction
    -   Feet can be elevated on a box or bench
    -   To make it easier, have feet on the floor with legs straight
    -   To make it even easier, bend the legs with feet on the floor

*  B1. Single Arm DB Bench Press

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/bUxfgjTklao]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/bUxfgjTklao/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   Do all reps on one side. Then the other.
    -   Do not hold any weight in the non working limb. Engage teh core to
      resist rotation.
    -   Maintain tension in the pecs at the bottom or the movement.

*  B2. Single Arm Standing Cable Row

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/mRk69bN2v7Q]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/mRk69bN2v7Q/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   Stand in a quarter squat position.
    -   Do all reps on one side, then the other.
    -   Engage the core to resist rotation.

*  C1. Dips

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/iKRV6yZzQuA]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/iKRV6yZzQuA/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   Controlled lowering until you feel some tension in the pectorals
    -   Keep elbows parallel, not caving in or flaring out
    -   If you cannot do dips and have no way off assisting yourself, do
      them with a bench as shown below

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/XwXGdC0K050]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/XwXGdC0K050/hqdefault.jpg]]

*  C2. Low Incline Paused Y to T

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/2Z-Ib02O8J0]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/2Z-Ib02O8J0/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   Initiate the movement at the shoulders. Thumbs face up to the Y
      position.
    -   Retract the shoulders fully and rotate to thumbs down as you bring
      the weight back to the T position.

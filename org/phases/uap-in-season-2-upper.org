*  B2. Pullovers

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/52c015jdieo]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/52c015jdieo/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   Keep elbows at approximately the same angle throughout the movement
    -   As you lower the weight, you should feel a slight stretch in your
      lower pectoral muscles
    -   Squeeze your chest to initiate the movement and bring the weight
      above your face.
    -   Keep your hips up and core engaged for the duration of the set.
    -   At the end of your set, place the weight on the bench beside you or
      drop it to the floor after lowering it behind your head.

*  C1. Bow and Arrow

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/Fq9Jlz261m4]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/Fq9Jlz261m4/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   if you imagine an archer, you will naturally do this exercise
      correctly
    -   stand up tall
    -   keep the elbow of the pulling arm up
    -   squeeze the shoulder blades together during the iso hold

*  C2. Band Internal Rotations

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/i-CwRCoXZD8]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/i-CwRCoXZD8/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   Keep elbow tight to the body.
    -   Keep shoulder retracted and chest up.
    -   Use a cable colums if you have no bands.  Alternatively you can use
      a light dumbbell and lie on your back.

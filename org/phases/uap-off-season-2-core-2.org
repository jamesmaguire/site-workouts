* A1. Kneeling Overhead Pallov Press
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/tGgkOlsd7Pc]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/tGgkOlsd7Pc/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - Knee closest to the cable column is on the floor
    - Attempt to hold the cable handle straight above your head without leaning in either direction.

* A2. Kneeling Reverse Half Chop
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/IR7smihfMTE]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/IR7smihfMTE/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - Knee closest to the cable column is on the floor
    - Look at the weight as you pull it across your body

* A3. One Arm One Leg Row
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/zVTQi0JZ6sA]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/zVTQi0JZ6sA/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - Keep hips level.
    - Resist rotation of the torso and hips as you pull.
    - This exercise may not feel difficult. You won't "feel the burn." That's ok. It's a total core exercise working on balance and core coordination.

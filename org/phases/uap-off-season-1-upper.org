* A2. Low Incline Prone Paused DB Row
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/XqkV8RL4iaM]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/XqkV8RL4iaM/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - really squeeze your shoulder blades together at the top as you hold for 2 counts

* B1. Pullovers
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/52c015jdieo]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/52c015jdieo/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - Keep elbows at approximately the same angle throughout the movement
    - As you lower the weight, you should feel a slight stretch in your lower pectoral muscles
    - Squeeze your chest to initiate the movement and bring the weight above your face.
    - Keep your hips up and core engaged for the duration of the set.
    - At the end of your set, place the weight on the bench beside you or drop it to the floor after lowering it behind your head.

* B2. 1 & 1/4 Semi-supinated grip Pull-ups
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/vE9L7GC4y8E]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/vE9L7GC4y8E/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - Grip the bar with palms facing one another
    - If you do not have a bar that allows this, grip with palms facing toward you
    - If you have no way to do assisted chinups, you can do lat pulldowns instead (though these are not quite as good)

* C1. Speed Push-ups
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/7lHuWdR6iQs]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/7lHuWdR6iQs/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - do as many pushups as possible. be explosive! don't pace yourself, go fast.

* C2. Incline Y to T Raise
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/2Z-Ib02O8J0]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/2Z-Ib02O8J0/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - Initiate the movement at the shoulders. Thumbs face up to the Y position.
    - Retract the shoulders fully and rotate to thumbs down as you bring the weight back to the T position.

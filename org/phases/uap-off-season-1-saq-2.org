These workouts are to be preceded by the [[./uap-warmups-linear-warmup.org][Linear Warmup]].

* A. Scissor Jumps

  {{{youtube(Bo_Rg5fhZMQ)}}}


  - Aim for maximum height
  - Feet should be shoulder width apart when landing.
  - With minimal ground contact time, jump up again into the next
    scissor.

* B. Power Skips for Distance

  - Get full extension of the hip as you skip forward and up.
  - Use your arms to add momentum

* C. Accelerate/Decelerate

  {{{youtube(j_9qk0MJ4Fw)}}}


  - Accelerate as quickly as possible to maximum speed.
  - Decelerate to walking by the time you get to the end point
  - Allow yourself to decelerate natually. do not force yourself to
    decelerate more quickly than you are capable.

* D. Low Squat Jumps

  {{{youtube(tiaf7MAyJ-w)}}}


  - Get your butt low but chest up, maintaining a neutral spine.
  - Think of lifting your knees quickly to your chest.
  - Bounce wtih minimal ground contact time for the duration.

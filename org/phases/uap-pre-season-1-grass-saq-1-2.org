These workouts are to be preceded by the [[https://www.ultyresults.com/products/ultimate-athlete-project/categories/89306/posts/231481][Nonlinear Warmup]].

* SAQ 1

  This day is all about Jumping.

** A. 2 Low Squat Jumps to Scissor Jumps
   {{{youtube(Mf7cZzcCyuc)}}}
  
   - Knees should be close to 90 degrees in the low squat jumps.
   - Get as high as possible in the scissor jumps with minimal ground contact times between jumps.
    
** B. Single Leg Bench Pushoff Jumps

   {{{youtube(zT_jMofv4V8)}}}

   -   Slam the working leg onto the bench and push yourself as high into
     the air as possible.
   -   You should move slightly forward with each jump to ensure maximal
     hip extension.

* SAQ 2

  This day is all about footwork.

** A. Jab Step and Cut

   {{{youtube(_6k3XvTzuak)}}}

   -   Focus on good quality of movement.
   -   Move more quickly and more powerfully as you become more
     comforatable with the movement pattern.
   -   Work on a strong push off the outside foot.

** B. Waltz and Cut

   {{{youtube(GD_f0hbzGcs)}}}

   -   Focus on good quality of movement.
   -   Move more quickly and more powerfully as you become more
     comforatable with the movement pattern.
   -   Work on movement of the hips and a strong push off the inside foot.

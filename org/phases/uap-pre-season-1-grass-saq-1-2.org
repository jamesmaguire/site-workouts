   These workouts are to be preceded by the [[https://www.ultyresults.com/products/ultimate-athlete-project/categories/89306/posts/231481][Nonlinear Warmup]].

* SAQ 1

    This day is all about Jumping.

* A. 2 Low Squat Jumps to Scissor Jumps [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/Mf7cZzcCyuc]] [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/Mf7cZzcCyuc/hqdefault.jpg]] -   Knees should be close to 90 degrees in the low squat jumps. -   Get as high as possible in the scissor jumps with minimal ground contact times between jumps.
* B. Single Leg Bench Pushoff Jumps

     [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/zT_jMofv4V8]]
     [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/zT_jMofv4V8/hqdefault.jpg]]

     -   Slam the working leg onto the bench and push yourself as high into
       the air as possible.
     -   You should move slightly forward with each jump to ensure maximal
       hip extension.

* SAQ 2

    This day is all about footwork.

* A. Jab Step and Cut

     [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/_6k3XvTzuak]]
     [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/_6k3XvTzuak/hqdefault.jpg]]

     -   Focus on good quality of movement.
     -   Move more quickly and more powerfully as you become more
       comforatable with the movement pattern.
     -   Work on a strong push off the outside foot.

* B. Waltz and Cut

     [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/GD_f0hbzGcs]]
     [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/GD_f0hbzGcs/hqdefault.jpg]]

     -   Focus on good quality of movement.
     -   Move more quickly and more powerfully as you become more
       comforatable with the movement pattern.
     -   Work on movement of the hips and a strong push off the inside foot.

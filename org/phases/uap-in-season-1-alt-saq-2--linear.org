* A. Lateral Shuffle to Sprint
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/hX3TKqIFCbM]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/hX3TKqIFCbM/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - One rep = 7 yard shuffle, 20 yards sprint
    - Rest 60 seconds between reps
    - Alternate directions each rep
    - 3 Reps facing each direction
    - After the last rep, rest 2 minutes

* B. 3-Point Start
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/j76bJSWSKzg]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/j76bJSWSKzg/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - Rest 60 seconds between reps
    - 3 Reps facing each direction
    - After the last rep, rest 2 minutes

* C. Low Squat Jump
    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/tiaf7MAyJ-w]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/tiaf7MAyJ-w/hqdefault.jpg]]

    - Try to get your hands lower than your knees while maintaining a neutral spine.
    - Rapidly bring your knees to your chest for 10 seconds
    - Rest 60 seconds
    - Repeat once more.

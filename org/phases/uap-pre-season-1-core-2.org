    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/tGgkOlsd7Pc]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/tGgkOlsd7Pc/hqdefault.jpg]]

      leaning in either direction.

*  A2. 1 & 1/4 Wood Chop

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/COCqjeny0Ds]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/COCqjeny0Ds/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   (start light 8-10 each side. Add one rep each week.)
    -   explosive chop. quarter rep at the end
    -   slowly lower the weight back to starting position.
    -   look at your hands as you move

* A3. Plank Walk

     [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/_QmurtmSi7s]]
     [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/_QmurtmSi7s/hqdefault.jpg]]

     -   Keep shoulders, hips, and ankles in a line
     -   Walk five paces sideways and back twice for each repetition.
     -   One direction, lead with the hands (hand, foot, hand, foot). the
       other direction lead with the feet.

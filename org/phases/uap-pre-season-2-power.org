   For the power circuit, exercises should be done as quickly as possible
   while maintaining good form. Keep your mental focus during this workout
   and get at it!
*

*  A1. Single Arm DB High Pull

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/qagm6U0CDLA]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/qagm6U0CDLA/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   It's all in the hips.
    -   DB should follow a straight line trajectory upwards
    -   Switch arms at the 15 second mark

*  A2. DB High Pull to Press

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/I-jYhdJqAPk]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/I-jYhdJqAPk/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   Catch the weight at the shoulder in a semisupinated position
    -   Push the weight up as you stand up from a quarter squat position

*  A3. Squat to Press

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/1O8jb9KLtf4]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/1O8jb9KLtf4/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   Find a rhythm and move quickly
    -   Aim for parallel in the squats
    -   The press should move continuously with momentum coming from the
      squat

* Alternate between following this circuit up with either:

*  Day 1. Backward Lunges

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/BYbEvMbOfMI]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/BYbEvMbOfMI/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   Step back with one leg. Do not let your knee touch the ground
    -   Explosively step forward again using primarily the front leg
    -   Keep the back foot off the ground when you stand up again if
      possible
    -   Do all reps on one leg first and then the other

*  Day 2. Russian Step Ups

    [[https://www.youtube.com/embed/jSnoZlYUttg]]
    [[https://img.youtube.com/vi/jSnoZlYUttg/hqdefault.jpg]]

    -   find a rhythm, be explosive, drive the knee up!
    -   Use the foot on the bench to initiate and drive the movement. Don't
      "bounce" off the foot landing on the ground.

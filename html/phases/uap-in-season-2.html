This schedule assumes weekend practices or tournaments.

Lower

A1. Front Foot Elevated Paused Split Squat

https://www.youtube.com/embed/NpzqPBBn1qo

  • push through the heel of your front foot
  • hips should move straight up and down
  • pause for 2 seconds at the bottom position
  • keep shoulders back and chest tall

A2. Split Squats jumps for height

https://www.youtube.com/embed/Bo_Rg5fhZMQ

  • Get low but do not leg the back knee smash into the ground
  • Jump as high as possible and switch which leg is forward mid-flight
  • Arms can do whatever but using them as you would if running will likely feel most natural

B1. Weighted Hip Thrust

https://www.youtube.com/embed/tWd5ikvnaPc

  • Weight rests approximately on hip flexors
  • Place a sweatshirt or folded up yoga mat between your hips and the weight for added comfort.
  • Heels should be directly under the knees at the top position.
  • Focus on squeezing your glutes. Keep back straight, not arched.

B2. Goblet Squats

https://www.youtube.com/embed/JyigpzdH3ps

  • Initiate the movement with hips going back
  • Squat as deep as mobility allows while maintaining spine neutrality

C1. Russian Step Up

https://www.youtube.com/embed/jSnoZlYUttg

  • find a rhythm, be explosive, drive the knee up!
  • Use the foot on the bench to initiate and drive the movement. Don't "bounce" off the foot landing on the ground.

C2. Standing Hip Flexion

https://www.youtube.com/embed/VcJsZ--kcSY

  • Place on foot on a box or bench that causes your knee to be close to 90 degrees.
  • Keep your hips level for the duration of this exercise!
  • Pull your leg straight up (it won't go very far) and hold for three seconds before lowering it back to the bench.

Upper

A1. Paused Incline DB Bench Press

https://www.youtube.com/embed/k4mI7a_tSbw

  • during the pause at the bottom your pectorals should feel tension

A2. Speed Push Ups

https://www.youtube.com/embed/7lHuWdR6iQs

  • do as many pushups as possible. be explosive! don't pace yourself, go fast.

B1. Pull Ups with Wide, Supine Grip

  • Start with grip wider than shoulder width, palms facing toward you
  • Squeeze shoulder blades together at the top*

B2. Pullovers

https://www.youtube.com/embed/52c015jdieo

  • Keep elbows at approximately the same angle throughout the movement
  • As you lower the weight, you should feel a slight stretch in your lower pectoral muscles
  • Squeeze your chest to initiate the movement and bring the weight above your face.
  • Keep your hips up and core engaged for the duration of the set.
  • At the end of your set, place the weight on the bench beside you or drop it to the floor after lowering it behind your head.

C1. Bow and Arrow

https://www.youtube.com/embed/Fq9Jlz261m4

  • if you imagine an archer, you will naturally do this exercise correctly
  • stand up tall
  • keep the elbow of the pulling arm up
  • squeeze the shoulder blades together during the iso hold

C2. Band Internal Rotations

https://www.youtube.com/embed/i-CwRCoXZD8

  • Keep elbow tight to the body.
  • Keep shoulder retracted and chest up.
  • Use a cable colums if you have no bands. Alternatively you can use a light dumbbell and lie on your back.

SAQ 1 & 2

SAQ 1:

A. Low squat jumps to split squat and 15 yard sprint

https://www.youtube.com/embed/077_AiKQD9E

  • concentrate on really getting the knees UP on the low squat jumps
  • EXPLODE as high as possible on the split squat jump
  • land as softly as possible in a low split squat position and explode forward into a sprint

B. 6 tuck jumps

https://www.youtube.com/embed/uCgaMLjr20I

  • Bring knees UP to the chest, not the chest down to the knees.
  • Use your arms to incrrease your jump height
  • Land softly and minimize ground contact time
  • Attempt to land in the same spot from which tou took off.
